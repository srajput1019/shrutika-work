What's real vs. illustrative. The company scale this cockpit is sized for — 400+ people, 1,200 customers, post–Series B — and the idea of tracking rep adoption of Nooks' own outbound product (Nooks selling to teams like HubSpot and Deel) come from Nooks' own public framing. Why this shape. Nooks frames its product as human judgment with AI execution; a Chief of Staff owns the judgment-heavy problems that fall between teams as a company scales. Built by someone who has made leadership legible before — UnitedHealthcare exec dashboards that embedded human oversight at every decision point, and Swaayata, where the whole design was instrumenting the moments that actually matter and making every autonomous action visible, reversible, and explainable.
