Why this is the product problem, not a feature

HiddenLayer's edge is coverage: AIDR, Model Scanner, and Automated Red Teaming surface adversarial activity across the MLOps pipeline and tag it to ATLAS. The unsolved part is the last mile — turning a technique ID into a decision a non-ML defender can make and a fix an owner can ship, with the clock visible the whole way. Get that right and the platform's findings become operational instead of merely correct.

  • Legibility toggle — the same detection, read as a SOC analyst vs. an ML engineer. The translation is the product surface a TPM owns.
  • Human-in-the-loop by default — every response is reversible and logged; auto-remediate is a deliberate, bounded choice, not the silent default.
  • Time-to-fix as the north-star metric — detection accuracy is table stakes; MTTR is what a security buyer renews on.
